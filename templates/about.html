Company Overview

WELCOME TO KRISHAT PHARMA INDUSTRIES LTD.

A Brand to reckon with in the Health & Pharmaceutical Industry.

Krishat Pharma Industries Ltd is a pharmaceutical drug manufacturing company in Ibadan, Oyo state and headquartered at Lagos state. From past 8 years, company has been developing and manufacturing pharmaceutical products in Nigeria and selling and distributing these in over 50 different locations in West-African region.

We are the first soft gelatin products producers in Nigeria with optimum production capacity of over 50 million capsules/month. Operating from a fully C-GMP Facility in Oyo state, we have a wide distribution network that is second to none and runs across the nation.
